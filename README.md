# Digital Trading Albums

A safe, time-boxed collectible experience for education and promotion

## What it is

This is a digital trading album platform inspired by classic sticker and trading card albums — redesigned for modern educational and promotional use.

Participants collect, trade, and complete albums over a short, fixed period (weeks, not years).
Each campaign runs as a self-contained season, then resets.

There is no money, no chat, and no long-term asset ownership.

## What it is not

- **Not a marketplace**

- **Not a crypto or NFT product**

- **Not a social network**

- **Not a gambling mechanic**

- **Not a permanent collectible system**

This is a controlled engagement tool, not an economy.

## How it works (at a glance)

- Each campaign runs for a fixed duration

- Participants receive random cards automatically

- Cards are placed into visual album pages

- Missing cards are clearly visible

- Duplicates can be traded using simple, rule-based exchanges

- When the campaign ends, the album closes and resets

The focus is on completion, participation, and interaction — not accumulation.

- Trading without risk

- Trading is intentionally constrained and safe:

- No money or pricing

- No free-text chat or messaging

- No direct negotiation

- All offers are structured and system-generated

- Clear value rules (e.g. a rare card may trade for multiple commons)

This allows users to practice decision-making and negotiation without exposure to abuse, scams, or financial risk.
